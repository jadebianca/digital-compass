What is

Digital Compass?

Digital Compass is an educational website dedicated to helping students and internet users understand cybercrimes and stay safe in the digital world. It provides well-researched articles, practical guides, and useful resources that explain different online threats such as phishing, hacking, identity theft, scams, and cyberbullying.

Our

Mission

Digital Compass aims to educate and empower students and internet users by providing reliable information, practical guides, and awareness about cybercrimes and online safety.

Our

Vision

Digital Compass envisions becoming a trusted educational platform that promotes cybersecurity awareness and digital responsibility for everyone.

People Behind Digital Compass

Meet the Team

With the goal of creating a reliable and educational platform on cybercrime and online safety, Digital Compass is composed of 10 BSIT students from the University of the East-Manila. They work together to develop a user-friendly platform that helps students and the wider community stay safe online.
